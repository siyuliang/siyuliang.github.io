Welcome!👋

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Computational Linguistics at the [University of Washington](https://linguistics.washington.edu/) advised by [Gina-Anne Levow](https://faculty.washington.edu/levow/) and [Richard Wright](https://depts.washington.edu/phonlab/rawright.php). My research interests lie broadly in phonetics and speech processing, including making use of speech cues to inform language models, low-resource speech technology, and speech variation. 

This is still a work under construction, so please check back for updates!
